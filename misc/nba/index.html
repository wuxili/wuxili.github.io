About Us

Foo Player contains a searchable database that allows you to search for any NBA players' performance in regular seasons from year 1985 to year 2013. The data on this site were gathered from here and images were found here. All content on this site is meant for non-commercial/educational use only.

Foo Player was created by Wuxi Li, Hao Lyu, Shao Yang, and Ruoying Li as part of the INF 385M Introduction to Databases course at the UT Austin School of Information. We wish to thank our professor Stan Gunn for his help with programming and SQL.

Basic Functions

Foo Player focuses on NBA players' performance in regular seasons. The following are the functions we have:

  • Search for a player's average performance by name for each season. The search results contain the player's basic stats.
  • Search for players by performance. The search results show all the players who satisfy your search requirements.
  • Search for a specific player and show his performance in a scatter plot.
  • Search for two specific players and compare their performances in a radar chart.
