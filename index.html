About

I am currently a Ph.D. student at University of Texas at Austin, working with David Z. Pan. Prior to coming to UT, I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University with B.S. in Microelectronics in 2013.

I do research in Electronic Design Automation (EDA). I like developing tools to increase the productivity of circuit designers, improve the quality of modern VLSI systems and overcome chip design challenges.
